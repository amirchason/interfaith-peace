## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Content Risks

**Risk 1: Theological Inaccuracy**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** High (credibility loss)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Mandatory fact-checking by scholars from each tradition
  - Cite primary sources only
  - Use provisional language ("Many scholars believe...")
  - Include multiple perspectives

**Risk 2: Cultural Insensitivity**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** Very High (reputational damage)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Sensitivity readers from each tradition
  - Avoid stereotypes
  - Respect sacred texts
  - Acknowledge complexity

**Risk 3: Political Controversy (Especially Article 6 on Israel)**
- **Probability:** High
- **Impact:** Very High (potential backlash)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Focus ONLY on historical and scriptural
  - Avoid all current politics
  - Multiple scholarly reviews
  - Legal review before publishing
  - Clear disclaimer about scholarly, non-political approach

### SEO Risks

**Risk 4: Google Algorithm Updates**
- **Probability:** High (2-3 major updates/year)
- **Impact:** Medium (ranking fluctuations)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Focus on high-quality content (survives updates)
  - Diverse keyword portfolio
  - Strong E-E-A-T signals
  - Regular content updates

**Risk 5: Slow Growth / Low Initial Traffic**
- **Probability:** High (new domain)
- **Impact:** Medium (delayed ROI)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Realistic expectations (12-18 month horizon)
  - Focus on long-tail keywords first
  - Build backlinks aggressively
  - Leverage social media

**Risk 6: Competitor Copying Content**
- **Probability:** Medium
- **Impact:** Low (we have first-mover advantage)
- **Mitigation:**
  - Original analysis and unique angles
  - Regular content updates
  - Build brand reputation
  - Monitor for plagiarism
